About Me

Hello world! My name is Kianna Willmore. I am an 18 year old student attending Algonquin College. This my 1st year attending, and I am excited to join clubs such as rock climbing, gaming, and art. I hope to learn lots of useful skills during my two year program, including how to use Adobe programs, and code in Visual Studio.

Some of my favourite hobbies include swimming, skateboarding, drawing and painting. I find art and fitness to be very relaxing, since it helps me feel at ease and clear my mind of stress. My first ever drawing tablet was given to me on my 15th brithday, and I was very excited to explore the world of digital art. Since getting my first tablet, I've been trying my hand at 2D animation, and illustration. I believe that the best career for me would involve art, travel, and creativity.
